Achievements

  • Confirmation that anyone can easily digitalize the documents that requires an audit trail without changing the existing MFP’s user experience.
  • Confirmation that the trusted data infrastructure to achieve the above can be built at a low cost.(compared to the establishing the data infrastructure from scratch, decrease the cost by about 300 million yen (based on our calculation))
  • Confirmation of the possibility of deploying a similar infrastructure to a wide variety of devices (POS system handles payment information, network camera handles flow of people, medical device handles vital data etc.)
