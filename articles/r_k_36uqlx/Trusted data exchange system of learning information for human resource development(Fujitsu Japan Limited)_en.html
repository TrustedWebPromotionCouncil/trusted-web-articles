This page shows the "Trusted data exchange system of learning information for human resource development" exploried and tested by Fujitsu Japan Limited, in the 2022 "Use Case Demonstration Project of the Trusted Web".

Please click on the links at the bottom of this page to learn more about the details of the demonstration.

Current issues and What Trusted Web Solves



Data to verify

Achievements

Proof of academic achievement including the ability of students that is difficult to prove

  • By having other people who can be trusted prove the specific contents and abilities of individual courses that are difficult to prove, students' achievements can be accurately linked to the company. As a result, it becomes possible to provide the optimum employment support for each individual.
  • It has become possible to prove the academic achievement and ability of other than the result evaluation (volunteer activity and club activity) which was difficult to prove in the job hunting until now.

Business Model Review

  • It has been found that social needs are higher for certifications related to activities and skills that are not yet systematized, rather than certificates such as transcripts, which are already issued as papers.
  • With regard to the granularity of registering activities and skills adopted in this demonstration, it was demonstrated that students could easily register and evaluate activities and skills without hesitation, and that they could appropriately select activities and skills to submit to companies.
