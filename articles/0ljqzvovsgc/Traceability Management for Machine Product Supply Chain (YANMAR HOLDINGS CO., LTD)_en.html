This page shows the "Traceability Management for Machine Product Supply Chain" exploried and tested by YANMAR HOLDINGS CO., LTD, in the 2022 "Use Case Demonstration Project of the Trusted Web".

Please click on the links at the bottom of this page to learn more about the details of the demonstration.

Current issues and What Trusted Web Solves


Data to verify

  • Validate machine signature to verify legitimacy of the data contained in repair request from repair shop to the manufacturer. In particular, validate information (name of the repair shop, timeframe etc.) contained in machine signature and check the legitimacy of the request.
  • Confirm the legitimacy of exchanged data by validating every sender’s signature and conducting receiver’s verification.


Achievements

Targeting generated data in the machine product supply chain, we selected the scene of repairing machine, conducted scheme planning and prototyping for the safe data exchanges among stakeholders. The achievement is following:

  • Scheme planning and prototyping in the repair scene of machine product
  • Provisioning identity to things（machine）
  • Pairing of things (machine）with legal entity/person ：linking things and their responsible entities
  • Ensuring traceability of things (machine）：DID1 of things remains the same throughout the product lifecycle
  • Data disclosure control based on evidence verification：verification of data disclosure target and its basis
  • Limitation of the visibility scope of identity ：limitation of the visibility of user information
