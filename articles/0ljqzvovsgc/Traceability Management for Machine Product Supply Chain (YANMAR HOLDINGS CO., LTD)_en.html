Achievements

Targeting generated data in the machine product supply chain, we selected the scene of repairing machine, conducted scheme planning and prototyping for the safe data exchanges among stakeholders. The achievement is following:

  • Scheme planning and prototyping in the repair scene of machine product
  • Provisioning identity to things（machine）
  • Pairing of things (machine）with legal entity/person ：linking things and their responsible entities
  • Ensuring traceability of things (machine）：DID1 of things remains the same throughout the product lifecycle
  • Data disclosure control based on evidence verification：verification of data disclosure target and its basis
  • Limitation of the visibility scope of identity ：limitation of the visibility of user information
