This page shows the "Trust verification of service eligibility and provided data in virtual reality space" exploried and tested by NRI Digital, Ltd., in the 2022 "Use Case Demonstration Project of the Trusted Web".

Please click on the links at the bottom of this page to learn more about the details of the demonstration.

Current issues and What Trusted Web Solves



Data to verify

  • Services provided on a virtual space need a user’s identification to check one’s eligibility based on laws such as Act on Prevention of Transfer of Criminal Proceeds.
  • In order to perform identity verification correctly, the authenticity of the credentials is required. Specifically, Information from Issuer (credential issuer) should be made into a VC, then the user submit it to the service provider on a virtual space, and the service provider verify the VC’s authenticity in the virtual space.

Achievements

  • We studied privacy protection issue specific to Metaverse. We learned it requires means to satisfy both verification of users credentials and their protection of privacy in Metaverse.


  • We studied monetize issue to spread the idea of Trusted Web. The result is following 2 items.
    • We need a monetizing model for Issuer who is the service provider to issue Trust information.
    • Even if the issuer passes the temporary information usage fee to the verifier, without a system to ensure data traceability for the issued Trust information, Issuer does not generate revenue for issuing Trust for that information at a certain cost.
