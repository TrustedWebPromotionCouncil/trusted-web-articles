Achievements

  • We created key pairs to prove credentials and implemented the public key to VDR as well as students DID. Companies verified students identity by them.
  • BBS signature (zero-knowledge proof1) enabled students to create original academic certificate based on their preferences and policies by picking up the required information from multiple source without tampering and submit them to companies.
