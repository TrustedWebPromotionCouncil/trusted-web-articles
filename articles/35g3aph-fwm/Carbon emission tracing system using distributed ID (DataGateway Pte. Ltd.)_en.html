Achievements

  • Through the demonstration, it was found that the combination with decentralized storage is more advantageous than the combination with blockchain in terms of loss tolerance and security for proving non-falsification data.
  • By combination of distributed ID and ZKP(zero-knowledge proof)/secure computation, personal privacy information and organizational confidential information are accurately recorded, computed, and visualized without the risk of falsification, while still being protected. Since data is used in encrypted form, data handling is secure even across multiple organizations and companies, enabling even companies that were previously reluctant to participate in greenhouse gas tracing to participate.
  • Now that we have demonstrated this as an implemented form of credible primary data in the GHG protocol*, we were able to begin working with industry associations to encourage international standardization of this format.* GHG protocol: standard for calculation of GHG (green house gas) emission and its report
